Vector Art and Layering Practice using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator

As a token of appreciation to content creator BlueRyai, I wanted to incorporate one of his interests and create something he'd be proud of. Combine several different design elements and LOTS of artistic study, I decided placing him in his favorite video game would be of par. Therefore, I created a streamer card for him and kept the color palette consistent with the original inspiration.
