XG Magazine Promo

Layering and Texture in Adobe Photoshop

As a fan project for musical group XG, I decided to come up with a concept that best fits the members image and branding! XG is known for their Y2K concept and their seemingly good music. To better utilize this, I incorporated it into a pink and blue promotional Instagram post for an upcoming fan magazine, which features all of the members in their best era. The member featured here is Cocona!!
