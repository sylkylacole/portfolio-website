Yeong-ji Y2K TV Design

Experimentation with 3D elements, layering, and doodles using Adobe Photoshop

Around the time when I was beginning to become interested in 3D graphics and models, I experimented with combining the 3D and 2D sphere together through placing a 2D image (Yeong-Ji) and 3D elements (the text, TV, hearts, lines, etc etc) together on one composition. For a first project dabble into 3D design I was VERY proud of how it turned out and have since delved deeper into using 3D softwares such as Blender to make stunning 3D pieces!!
