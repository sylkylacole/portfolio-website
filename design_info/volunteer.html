Volunteer Match-Up Services

UI/UX Design Project for freeCodeCamp in Figma [open in new tab for better view :3]

In March 2026, I had the pleasure of designing the front end UI for Violet Carnetion's cohort project! After much discussion, as leader of the front end design team, we decided to all come up with our own respective designs and combine all of them in the final project. Presented is the original design I created that directly aligned with the projects development. This website is for those who are either seeking an organization to volunteer at (volunteer view), or an organization seeking a volunteer to add to their company for a good cause (organization view). The color scheme was chosen with the feeling of invitation and warmth in mind, and green was a color that resonated with this sentiment.
