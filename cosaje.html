Connie Sasha Jean Magazine Cover

Layout and Composition Practice using Adobe InDesign

This was the first project I completed during my free time in the summer of this year! The growth since then is evident, but this is a piece I am definitely proud of!! This was a commissioned design for the Attack on Titan series that captured an AU where the characters are all acting for the roles they play in the actual anime/manga!! With heavy focus on layout and composition, I kept a monochromatic color scheme and played around with vector shapes and text placement.
