Gina De Bosschere Album Concept

Color correction and 3D experimentation using Adobe Photoshop

The second installment of creating debut album covers for A2K contestants, Gina's design was very special!! Following the release of her actual debut song "Freedom", I wanted to completely flip the switch and contrast the graceful concept with something a lot more hardcore. With heavy color exposure and a duotone color palette, I wanted to express that upfront confidence in her works.
