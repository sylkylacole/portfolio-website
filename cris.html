Cristina Lopez of A2K Debut Album Concept

Layering Practice using Adobe Photoshop

After the final episode of A2K aired on YouTube, I was excited to be able to create concepts for members who didn't make the final girl group. My favorite of the bunch was Cristina Lopez, part time dancer and full time comedian! Through her progress in the show, I believed she'd absolutely GLOW if she did a Y2K concept, so I decided to bring my idea to life!! Her reaction was priceless and I was happy to be able to share that with her!
